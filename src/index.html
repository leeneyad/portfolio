Meal Mind

This project is a Python and Web-based application designed to support children’s health. The system collects key information such as a child’s age, weight, and height, and then generates personalized breakfast suggestions. The recommendations are tailored based on available medical data and take into account specific conditions such as diabetes, ensuring that each child receives a meal that is both nutritious and suitable for their health needs. The goal of the project is to make healthy eating more accessible and engaging for children, while also helping parents and healthcare providers ensure balanced nutrition.
